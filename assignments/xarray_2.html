Assignment: More Xarray with El Ni√±o-Southern Oscillation (ENSO) Data#

Here will will calculate the NINO 3.4 index of El Nino variabillity and use it to analyze datasets.

First read this page from NOAA. It tells you the following:

  • The Ni√±o 3.4 region is defined as the region between +/- 5 deg. lat, 170 W - 120 W lon.

  • Warm or cold phases of the Oceanic Ni√±o Index are defined by a five consecutive 3-month running mean of sea surface temperature (SST) anomalies in the Ni√±o 3.4 region that is above the threshold of +0.5¬∞C (warm), or below the threshold of -0.5¬∞C (cold). This is known as the Oceanic Ni√±o Index (ONI).

(Note that ‚Äúanomaly‚Äù means that the seasonal cycle, also called the ‚Äúclimatology‚Äù has been removed.)
